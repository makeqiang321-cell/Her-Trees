The Series

Three Her Trees Games

Each game builds on the last — more rooms, deeper logic, sharper challenges. All of them share the same hand-drawn monochrome look, the same silence, the same quiet satisfaction of figuring it out alone. You can play every Her Trees game online in a browser with no download.

Free

Her Trees: First Puzzle

Where it all started. One room, a handful of objects, about half an hour. Her Trees: First Puzzle is completely free — play it online in your browser. No download, no signup, no ads. A single Her tree puzzle teaches you everything: drag, overlap, discover. By the time the first flower blooms, you'll know whether this series clicks for you.

Her Trees 2

Her Trees: The Puzzle House

The second game — what players call Her Trees 2. More rooms than the first, puzzles that dig deeper into spatial thinking. Objects overlap in less obvious ways. You can play Her Trees: The Puzzle House online through the free browser demo, no download needed. Full version on Steam. If First Puzzle teaches the language, The Puzzle House makes you fluent.

43 Puzzles

Her Trees: Puzzle Dream

The biggest Her Trees puzzle game. 43 puzzles spread across a dreamlike space. Drag, layer, rotate — paper over paper, branch under leaf — until hidden letters surface. Type the code, a flower blooms on the tree, next room. Steam and iOS with a free online demo. The Her tree series culminates here.

Answers

Common Questions

Which Her Trees game should I start with?

Start with Her Trees: First Puzzle. It's free, takes about half an hour, and teaches you the core mechanic. If you enjoy it, jump into Puzzle Dream for 43 puzzles of the full experience. The Puzzle House sits in between — more rooms than First Puzzle, not as sprawling as Puzzle Dream. None of the games share a story, so order doesn't matter.

What's the difference between The Puzzle House and Puzzle Dream?

Her Trees: The Puzzle House has around 20 puzzles and takes 1-2 hours. It refines the formula from First Puzzle — more objects per room, trickier overlaps, deeper spatial thinking. Her Trees: Puzzle Dream is the largest with 43 puzzles and takes 1.5-3 hours. It introduces new visual tricks like 3D occlusion and complex layering that weren't in the earlier games. Puzzle Dream is the polished, definitive experience.

Can I play all three games without downloading anything?

Her Trees: First Puzzle is fully playable for free in your browser. Her Trees: The Puzzle House has a free browser demo — you can play it online right now. Her Trees: Puzzle Dream also has a browser demo. The full versions of Puzzle House and Puzzle Dream are on Steam. No downloads needed for the demos — just open and play.

No text. No tutorial. Just you and the room.
